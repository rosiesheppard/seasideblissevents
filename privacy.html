Privacy Policy

How Seaside Bliss Events handles enquiries and personal information.

Last updated: May 2026

Seaside Bliss Events respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect personal information submitted through our website or by email, telephone or direct enquiry.

Information we collect

We may collect your name, email address, telephone number, event date, guest numbers, budget range and any details you choose to share about your wedding, celebration or enquiry.

How we use your information

We use your information to respond to your enquiry, provide information about our services, prepare quotes, arrange consultations and manage bookings.

Lawful basis

We usually process enquiry information because it is necessary to take steps before entering into a contract, or because we have a legitimate interest in responding to potential clients.

Sharing your information

We do not sell your information. Where necessary, we may share relevant details with trusted suppliers only when this is required to support your event and with appropriate care.

How long we keep information

Enquiry information is kept only for as long as reasonably needed for communication, booking administration, legal, accounting or business record purposes.

Your rights

You may ask to access, correct or delete your personal information. You can contact us at info@seasideblissevents.co.uk.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Seaside Bliss Events at info@seasideblissevents.co.uk.
