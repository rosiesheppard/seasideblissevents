Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Who We Are

Seaside Bliss Events is a beachside events venue based in Herne Bay Pier, Herne Bay, Kent. We can be reached at info@seasideblissevents.co.uk.

2. What Data We Collect

When you submit an enquiry via our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and the message you send. We do not collect any other personal data without your explicit consent.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to respond to your enquiry and communicate with you about your event. We do not sell, share, or use your data for marketing without your consent.

4. Third-Party Services

Our enquiry forms are processed via Formspree. Please refer to Formspree's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.

5. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or delete any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@seasideblissevents.co.uk.

6. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies. Google Fonts are loaded from Google's CDN, which may set cookies subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at info@seasideblissevents.co.uk.
