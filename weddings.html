Frequently Asked Questions

Can we hold both the ceremony and reception at the venue?

Yes — our venue is set up to host both your ceremony beneath the floral arch and a relaxed reception by the sea.

How many guests can attend?

We accommodate intimate weddings through to larger gatherings. Contact us with your guest count and we'll advise on the best layout.

Is the floral arch included in all wedding packages?

The floral arch with white and blush roses is a signature feature of our wedding setup and is included as standard.

How do we book a DJ or photographer?

Both are available as add-ons. Simply mention them when you enquire and we'll arrange everything.

Is the venue available year-round for weddings?

Yes — the venue is available throughout the year. We'll work with you to create the ideal setup for any season.
