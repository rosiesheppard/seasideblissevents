About Us

A Venue Unlike Anywhere Else

Nestled right by the sea, Seaside Bliss Events is a one-of-a-kind beachside hut venue built for celebrations that matter. Warm, characterful, and full of charm — it's the backdrop that makes your event stand out from every other your guests have attended.

From grand floral wedding ceremonies to joyful kids' birthday parties, we bring the magic — and handle every detail along the way. Catering, décor, entertainment, photography — all taken care of.
